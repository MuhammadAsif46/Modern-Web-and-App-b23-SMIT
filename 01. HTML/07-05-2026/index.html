Karachi

Karachi is famous as Pakistan’s largest city, financial capital, and "City of Lights". It is renowned for its bustling Clifton Beach, vibrant street food (especially Biryani and Nihari), and landmarks like Mazar-e-Quaid. As a major, diverse metropolis, it serves as the country’s primary industrial and entertainment hub.
